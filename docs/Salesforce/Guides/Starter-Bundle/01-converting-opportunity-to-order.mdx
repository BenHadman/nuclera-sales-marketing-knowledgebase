# Converting an Opportunity to an Order

Once you have received a PO from the customer you will need to compare the details on the PO with that of the Opportunity Record/ Quote.
This Guide takes you through the process of comparing the two docs, and if matching converting the Opportunity to an Order.

You will also need to perform some housekeeping tasks at this stage of the process.

## Shipping-level Accounts
The Shipping-level account is the Account record that you will use to associate to the order and treat as the main Account attached to this user-group/ starter bundle order.
The data-completeness of the Account attached to the Opportunity prior to receiving the PO will likely be very low.
You will need to either clone a new account and add into the account hierarchy or use the existing account attached to the Opportunity.

You will need to add the following information:
- The shipping and billing addresses outlined on the PO, 
- Add the shipping, billing and any other Contacts associated with the user group to the Account.
- Change the currency.

Once you have created your Shipping level account you can start the *Opportunity to Order Wizard*
