# Scheduling a Meeting inside Google Calendar using the Outreach Plugin

Scheduling a meeting using the outreach plugin makes sure that the meeting is recorded properly against KPIs plus it saves your time populating the meeting by using pre-defined templates.

### [1\. Go to your Google Calendar](https://calendar.google.com/calendar/u/0/r?tab=mc&pli=1)

![](https://dubble-prod-01.s3.amazonaws.com/assets/96032393-5bdc-48c7-b7f4-91bbf6f25c2d.png?0)

### [2\. Click on the slot you would like to arrange the meeting](https://calendar.google.com/calendar/u/0/r?tab=mc&pli=1)

![](https://d3q7ie80jbiqey.cloudfront.net/media/image/zoom/a1da54c6-4d53-4424-a282-980831d89924/1/77.316484451294/80.992230703664?0)

### [3\. Click on Add guests](https://calendar.google.com/calendar/u/0/r?tab=mc&pli=1)

Add your guests first so that Outreach can find the related information about them.

![](https://d3q7ie80jbiqey.cloudfront.net/media/image/zoom/41381d9e-b522-4f4c-86a6-dcd3e3e4842e/2.0029281062202/38.663125991821/57.971093820971?0)

### [4\. (Optional) Use a pre-defined template to populate the title, description, meeting length etc.](https://calendar.google.com/calendar/u/0/r?tab=mc&pli=1)

You can either make your own or request a standard template.

![](https://d3q7ie80jbiqey.cloudfront.net/media/image/zoom/be7bd809-13ac-4671-af85-7374e8980053/1.7370289459312/37.288411140442/27.467712136202?0)

### [5\. Associate an opportunity to the meeting](https://calendar.google.com/calendar/u/0/r?tab=mc&pli=1)

this should auto suggest the associated opportunity with a contact but if not search for it

![](https://d3q7ie80jbiqey.cloudfront.net/media/image/zoom/31034292-05e6-4d2d-ac09-e3dc5ed66be8/1.7370289459312/37.288411140442/38.024427724439?0)

### [6\. Save](https://calendar.google.com/calendar/u/0/r?tab=mc&pli=1)

Your meeting will show up against the Contact and Opportunity activity timelines

![](https://d3q7ie80jbiqey.cloudfront.net/media/image/zoom/93ab7f5b-8fab-49ea-9d2f-984865366ab1/1.71253928687/37.535805702209/92.842515679293?0)
